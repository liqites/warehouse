一、登陆
1. 登陆：
 POST "/api/v1/users/login/"
 
2. 登陆后调用
  GET "/api/v1/locations/get_all"
  GET "/api/v1/regexes/labels"

二、托清单
1. 刚进入页面：加载查看个人 
 GET "/api/v1/forklifts/get_by_time_and_state?state[]=0&state[]=1&state[]=2&state[]=3&state[]=4&type=0"
2. 点击查看全部：
 GET "/api/v1/forklifts/get_by_time_and_state?all=1&state[]=0&state[]=1&state[]=2&state[]=3&state[]=4&type=0"
